PERA — Personal Equity & Retirement Account

Build your retirement wealth. Keep more of it.

PERA is the only government-backed retirement vehicle in the Philippines offering complete tax-free growth. Every year you delay is a year of tax savings permanently lost.

  • Up to ₱100,000 tax deduction per year
    OFWs qualify for ₱200,000. Deductible directly from taxable income, reducing your annual tax bill.
  • Investment returns grow completely tax-free
    No capital gains tax. No final withholding tax. 100% of your growth stays in your account.
  • CGSI manages more PERA accounts than any independent broker
    As a BSP-accredited PERA Administrator, we offer the widest selection of PERA investment options.
